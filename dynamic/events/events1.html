Have mercy on the souls of the martyrs of Gaza

  • 25, MAI 2021
  • POSTED BY ADMIN

the Dynamic Club at the Faculty of Science in Monastir organized a pause for mercy on the souls of the martyrs of Gaza under the title “For your pure souls: the flames of our hearts are translated by the flames of our candles.” The stand included lighting candles for the souls of our righteous martyrs and giving the floor to the interventions of our colleagues on the issue of the nation, in addition to a "Dabka" show presented by CLUB FSM ARTS , whom we thank for giving the event a special character and luster.
